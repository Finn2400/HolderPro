Face indices refer to the triangle order in the posed mesh passed to the native
adapter. HolderPro uses a minimal 3MF container for painted jobs because its
explicit triangle order survives import without STL repair removing or
reordering facets. Records are optional, zero-based, unique, and may appear in
either state. `E` maps to PrusaSlicer's TriangleStateType::ENFORCER and `B` maps
to TriangleStateType::BLOCKER. The declared face count must match the loaded
mesh, so a stale or reordered painting fails closed instead of reaching another
face.
